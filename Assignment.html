The Best Basketball Players Ever

Michael Jordan

When people discuss who is the greatest basketball player ever, the athlete most people think of is Michael Jordan. He played in the NBA from the early 1980s through the early 2000s. He retired twice before his official third retirement while playing with the Washington Wizards. Jordan won 6 NBA championships and was the first internation basketball star.
